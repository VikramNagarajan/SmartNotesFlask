Help

To create a new SmartNote, simply click the button above. Then, either upload a PDF file of your lesson or paste the URL of an article or online text. Afterwards, use the filter options to customize your note file. Then, within minutes, a PDF file of your notes will be displayed along with a list of key vocabulary words.
