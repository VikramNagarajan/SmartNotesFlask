About

School has always been difficult, and here at SmartNotes, we know it isn't getting any easier. What's the problem? Why are students struggling with school? Well, put simply, students aren't equipped with the tools to study effectively... until now.

SmartNotes is your go-to place for all things studying! Simply upload a PDF of your textbook or paste in a link to an article, and our AI-powered alogrithims will provide easy-to-understand notes of the topic as well definitions to key vocabulary words!
